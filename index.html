Privacy & Security by Design

Your care, locations, memories, and will are encrypted and protected. You stay in control of what’s shared and when.

  • End‑to‑end encryption for sensitive content (will, messages, lessons).
  • Granular consent: you choose who sees what and under which conditions.
  • Zero‑knowledge vault for will & legacy content—only you hold the keys.
  • Emergency contact & executor mechanisms for verified after‑life delivery.
